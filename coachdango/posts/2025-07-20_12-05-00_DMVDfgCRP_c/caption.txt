Here’s your step-by-step guide to speeding up your metabolism:

Step 1: Become more NEAT (Non-Exercise Activity Thermogenesis)

This is a fancy way of saying to get at least 8 to 10k steps a day in whichever way you prefer.

Step 2: Strength training

Start by lifting heavy stuff. Muscle burns calories, even while you’re binge-watching Netflix. 

Step 3: Get 30 grams of protein or more in every meal.

This is like giving your body premium fuel, and your metabolism works harder to break it down. 

Step 4: Become a sleep athlete

Quit skipping sleep. Being tired messes with your hormones and makes your body hold onto fat like it’s some weird survival strategy. 

Final step: Drink water

Hydration keeps your body functioning smoothly and also acts as an appetite suppressant.

You can’t out-hack biology, but you can definitely work with it.

Follow these steps, and you’ll find your metabolism to be faster than you thought.
