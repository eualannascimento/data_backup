My dad is 78 years old, and he’s in great shape. The 2 exercises he does that keep him fit are weights and walking. 
 
Walking stimulates the brain and body while reducing stress. 
 
Weights help you maintain one of the most important longevity organs in your body, which is muscle. 
 
Make a habit of doing both, and your 80-year-old self will be thanking you one day.
