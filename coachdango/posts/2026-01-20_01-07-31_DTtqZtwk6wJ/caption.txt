Want a simple way to optimize your water intake? 
 
Put a pinch of sea salt for every liter of water you drink. 
 
Why? When you go pee, you’re pissing out minerals and electrolytes. 
 
The best way to replenish these is to add high-quality sea salt to your water. 
 
Sea salt is high in Magnesium, Potassium, Calcium, and Sodium. These minerals are key to muscle, brain, and heart health. 
 
Celtic Sea Salt and Himalayan Sea Salt are two types known to have high levels of these precious nutrients. 
 
Try this next time you drink water, and you may notice a slight difference.
