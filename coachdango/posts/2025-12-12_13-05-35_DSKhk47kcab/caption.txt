Your body is the only investment you can’t liquidate, replace, or upgrade later.

Every workout compounds. Every healthy meal builds interest. Every good night’s sleep pays dividends.

Most people treat their health like a credit card: max it out today, worry about the bill later.

The smart ones treat it like their retirement account, consistent deposits, no matter how small.

That 5am workout? That’s not discipline. That’s compound interest.
Skipping the processed junk? That’s not a restriction. That’s asset protection.
Prioritizing sleep? That’s not lazy. That’s portfolio management.

Your future self is either thanking you or suing you.

There’s no neutral.

- Dan
