I’m about to turn 46. Here’s what I’d tell my 36-year-old self:

Skip the alcohol. It takes 3 days for every night out.

Stop program-hopping. Pick one workout. Get stronger.

Eat to fuel, not to feel. Food isn’t therapy.

Walk daily. Your brain needs it more than your body.

Sleep is your superpower. Protect it like your bank account.

Cut the gossip, blame, and excuses. They’re cancer.

Drop your ego. It’s blocking everything good.

Surround yourself with people who make you uncomfortable. Comfort is death.

Show up when you don’t feel like it. That’s when it counts.

Age is perception. Start now.

So my question to you today is:

What would you tell your younger self?

- Dan
