Peanut butter isn’t steak. 

100g of peanut butter = 25g protein, 588 calories. 
100g of steak = 25g protein, 117 calories, plus iron, zinc, B vitamins, and creatine. 

Most peanut butter adds sugar and oils. 

Even vegans have better options: chickpeas, spelt, quinoa. 

Repeat after me: peanut butter ≠ protein.
