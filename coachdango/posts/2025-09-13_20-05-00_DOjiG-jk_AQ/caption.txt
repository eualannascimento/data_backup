Walking: My daily maintenance. Burns fat, clears my head, keeps joints smooth. 10k steps minimum.

Strength: My armor. Heavy lifts 3x/week. Bones get denser, metabolism speeds up, and everyday tasks become effortless.

Mobility: My insurance policy. 10 minutes daily. No more groaning when I tie my shoes.

Sprints: My youth serum. Once a week. Reminds my body it’s still an athlete.

Most guys my age are falling apart.

I’m getting faster.

Aging happens. Feeling old is a choice.

So my question to you today is:

Which workout type do you skip most?
