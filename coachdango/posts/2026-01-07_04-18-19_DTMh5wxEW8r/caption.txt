40 is not old. It’s just the beginning.

Society sold you a lie that your best years are behind you at 40.

The truth? You finally have:

Clarity on what matters. Resources to invest in yourself. Wisdom to avoid stupid mistakes. Patience to play the long game.

Your 20s were for learning. Your 30s were for building. Your 40s? That’s when it all clicks.

Most people peak after 40. Not before.

Stop acting like you’re winding down. You’re just getting started.

40 is young. Your mindset is the only thing that’s aging.

What would you do if 40 was just your warm-up?

- Dan
