Most people have been overfeeding themselves for so long that they forget what true hunger even feels like. 
 
What people think is hunger is actually boredom, stress, or another emotion they’re trying to numb. 
 
So next time you feel like snacking, try this: 
 
Drink 500 ml of water and then wait 20 minutes. 
 
If you’re hungry, go ahead and eat. 
 
If you’re not, it probably wasn’t hunger. 
 
What strategies do you use to avoid emotional eating?
