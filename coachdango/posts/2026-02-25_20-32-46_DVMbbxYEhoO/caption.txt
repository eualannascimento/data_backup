One of the best cheat codes in life?

Never get offended.

Train yourself to have thick skin.

Let people disagree. Let them talk.

Being easily offended means you’re easily manipulated.

One comment bothers you for two days.

For what? Nothing.

Want drama and poor mental health? Get offended easily.

Want peace, focus, and happiness? Let it slide.

Not being offended is a superpower.

It means you’re in control.

Not them.

Most people hand their peace away for free.

Don’t be like most people.

- Dan
