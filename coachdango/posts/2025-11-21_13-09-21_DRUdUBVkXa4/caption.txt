At 30, I thought I had time to fix things later. At 40, I realized “later” was now.

Most people over 30 are still operating on their 20-year-old habits. Still making excuses. Still waiting for the “right time.”

Your body doesn’t care about your excuses. Your metabolism already changed. Your recovery already slowed. Your margin for error already shrunk.

The habits that got you through your 20s are now actively working against you.

Every year you wait, the math gets worse.

Stop negotiating with your decline. Start building your comeback.

The best time to change was 10 years ago. The second best time is today.
