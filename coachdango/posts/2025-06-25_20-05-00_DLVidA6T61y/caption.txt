I’m 45 at the time of writing this and if I could sit down with my 25-year-old self here’s what I’d say…

Hey Dan,

I know right now you feel lost and afraid. You’re going nowhere and you’re using drugs and alcohol to escape the existential dread of a purposeless life.

I just want to let you know that it gets better from here.

At some point, you’ll get into great shape. You’ll have a wife and 2 children you absolutely love. You’ll live in an amazing town. You’ll have incredible friends. You’ll make more money in a day than you ever will working your corporate job.

Life is blessed and God is good.

If I were to give you any piece of advice it would be this:

Keep your body healthy. Prioritize exercise, food & sleep for your mental health.

Stop trying to numb your emotions with food, drugs, or alcohol. Deal with them head on or they become harder to handle later on.

Keep people around you who have good values and inspire you to become better.

Do some form of work you love doing. You’re going to be working for most of your life. Might as well enjoy it.

Be intentional about your life. Move towards your vision rather than be pulled by your past.

Lastly, if you follow this 3 step formula you will achieve success no matter what you choose:

1 - Show up
2 - Do the work
3 - Always look for ways to improve

You can’t go wrong following that for the rest of your life.

Chin up my friend. Life’s a journey. Enjoy every step.

Your friend,
Dan
