A fit body speaks more truth than an expensive car.

You can inherit money. You can lease luxury. You can fake wealth with debt.

But you can’t fake a fit body.

No trust fund gives you abs. No loan gets you strong. No inheritance builds your endurance.

Every muscle was earned. Every mile was run. Every rep was completed.

When someone’s fit at 40, 50, or 60, you’re looking at years of choices. Thousands of workouts. Millions of small decisions.

A car shows what you have. A fit body shows who you are.

One depreciates. The other appreciates.

Which are you building?

So my question to you today is:

What does your body say about you?

- Dan
