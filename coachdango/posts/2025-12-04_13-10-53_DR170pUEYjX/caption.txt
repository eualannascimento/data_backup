Last year, I was working with an entrepreneur named Van who wanted to drop 25 lbs off his body and increase his energy levels.

He was sick and tired of the yo-yo weight loss cycle and wanted to be a good example to his 2 children.

In 3 months, he dropped the 27 lbs, and I’m proud to say he’s kept it off since then.

Here’s how we did it, and literally, step-by-step, how you can do it too.

Step 1: Find ways to keep yourself honest.

1. We tracked weight 3x a week
2. We tracked inches bi-weekly
3. We took pictures every month

Having more numbers to track gives you a holistic view of the progress you’re making.

Step 2: Weights and walking

Weight training helps you build muscle and maintain your metabolism.

Walking helps decrease stress, increase energy, helps burn a few more calories & is less stressful on the body when it’s taking in fewer calories.

Step 3: Customizing meals to his lifestyle

We put him on a meal schedule that worked with his lifestyle.

85% of the calories he ate were single-ingredient, nutrient-dense foods.

Step 4: Optimizing sleep

We had him wear blue light blockers for at least 3 hours before going to sleep.

This helped entrain his rhythms, so he started feeling tired by the time he wanted to sleep.

Step 5: Coaching, Accountability and Community

If you’ve been struggling with your fitness goals, these are crucial.

Coaching, accountability and community are like adding rocket fuel to your goals.
