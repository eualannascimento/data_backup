If you want a better brain, don’t have a fat body.

Studies have shown that excess body fat is associated with alterations in brain regions responsible for emotional control and decision-making.

This includes reduced gray matter in the prefrontal cortex and abnormal growth in the amygdala and hippocampus. These lead to impaired emotional regulation and poor executive function.

Also, the very things that get your body in shape are the same things that improve your brain’s function.

Quality sleep, movement throughout the day, exercise, and getting enough nutrients from your diet all have a profound effect on improving your brain power.

Most people get in shape for their physical health, but the reality is that it will have a tremendous effect on their mental health.

So my question to you today is:

What do you think your brain would feel like in a healthier body?
