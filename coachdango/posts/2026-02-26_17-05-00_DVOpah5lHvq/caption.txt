The biggest shift I made at 46 had nothing to do with diet or workouts.

It was identity.

I created an alter ego: Dan Go 4.6.

The highest version of myself.

Then I started asking one question in every situation:

“What would Dan Go 4.6 do?”

Not what I feel like doing.

What he would do.

I stopped forcing habits through willpower.

I started acting from identity.

Here’s your challenge for the next 72 hours:

Act as your future self.

Make every decision from that place.

Stop being who you were.

Start being who you’re becoming.
