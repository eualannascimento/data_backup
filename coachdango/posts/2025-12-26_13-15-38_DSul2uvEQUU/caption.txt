The other day, I put out a survey to millions of followers on social media asking what they do to keep their bodies under 15% body fat. Here’s what they said...

1. They drink minimal to zero amounts of alcohol
2. They walk 10K steps
3. They lift weights
4. They eat minimal processed foods
5. They eat protein with every meal
6. They prioritize sleep

A lot of people came back and said that every day they eat similar meals, and this just makes sense.

While most people are seeking variety and entertainment with their food, people who keep themselves lean are okay with eating similar meals every day.

It’s less about genetics, and it’s more about the ability to fall in love with the boring, basic fundamentals.

Next time you think that losing weight or gaining lean mass is about some fad diet, check yourself and understand that it has more to do with habits and behavior than magic.
