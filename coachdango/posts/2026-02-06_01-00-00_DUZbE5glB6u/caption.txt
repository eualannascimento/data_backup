Here’s a simple system to incorporate good habits into your life:

1. Target a positive habit you want to add to your life.
2. Target a negative habit you want to eliminate.

For the positive habit, make it as small as possible. An example would be to meditate for 5 minutes a day instead of 20 right off the bat.

For the negative habit, try to make one that the positive habit can replace.

Do this every 6 months and be intentional about it.

After a few years, you might find your life has changed as a result of the compounding effect of good habits.
