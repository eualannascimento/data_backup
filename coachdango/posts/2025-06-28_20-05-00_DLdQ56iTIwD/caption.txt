When you’re in your 20s, you have time, youth, lack of responsibilities, and hormones on your side. 
 
When you get to 40+, the game changes. 
 
As long as you’re not a pro athlete, you can be in better shape at 40+ if you do the right things. 
 
Lift heavy objects, walk 8k steps a day, eat a nutrient-dense diet, stretch daily, drink water & get some sleep. 
 
So my question to you today is:

Are you taking steps towards a healthier future?
