Here’s a little weight loss secret we use with our coaching clients that I’m positive you’re not using:

It’s called the Theory of Constraints, and here’s how it works.

I had a client come to us one day, a bit frustrated.

He had no problems exercising, but he couldn’t stick to a diet and didn’t want to count calories.

Instead of telling him to suck it up, I intro’d him to the theory of constraints:

This system works on one constraint that holds the system back from achieving results.

His big issue was binge eating at night.

So we removed everything and focused our attention on fixing that habit.

We identified it, focused on it, he got support for it (aka. us), and we tracked our progress.

Fast forward a month later, and he’s not only fixed the habit he’s lost 10 lbs that he couldn’t shake off.

Are you feeling stuck with your body? Try this out and let me know how it works.
