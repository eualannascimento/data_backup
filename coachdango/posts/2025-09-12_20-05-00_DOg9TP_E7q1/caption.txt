My client treated fitness like a chore. Lost 20 lbs. Gained it all back. Three times.

“I hate every minute,” he admitted.

Then I told him: Make it a game.

Track your streaks. Beat yesterday’s score. Level up your lifts. Compete with yourself.

Now? Down 35 lbs for 2 years. Works out 6 days a week. Actually enjoys it.

Punishment never sticks. Play always does.

So my question to you today is:

How do you gamify your workouts?

- Dan
