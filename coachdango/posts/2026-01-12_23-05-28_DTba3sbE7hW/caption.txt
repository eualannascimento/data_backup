You can’t control how much you weigh. 
 
You can’t control how much body fat you have. 
 
You can’t even control how much muscle you build. 
 
What do you have control over? 
 
You have control over how often you workout. 
 
You have control over the foods you put into your body. 
 
You have control over when you go to bed. 
 
You will get much farther when you stop trying to control the outcomes and focus more on controlling your actions. 
 
You are not entitled to any outcomes. You are only entitled to the work.
