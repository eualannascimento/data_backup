I used to numb stress with food and alcohol.

Short term? It worked. Long term? It made everything worse.

Now my motto is simple: Push the body. Empty the mind.

Stressed? I walk. Anxious? I train hard.

This shift didn’t just make me healthier. It tamed my monkey brain.

Movement became my medicine. Sweat became my therapy.

When you stop numbing and start moving, everything changes.

Trade the bottle for the barbell. Trade the snack for the steps.

Your stress response shapes your life.

What’s your go-to when stress hits?
