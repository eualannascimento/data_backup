The secret to losing the belly without counting calories or dieting:

There is a concept in business called the Theory of Constraints, and here’s how it goes...

Every system has something holding it back from its best performance.

What we need to do is find the “constraint” and put all of our energy into fixing it before adding anything else.

An example would be binge eating at night:

1. Find out why you can’t control your eating.
2. Work to fix the issue.
3. Get support if needed.
4. Once fixed, find the next constraint.

Most people don’t need to count calories or follow fad diets.

They need to fix their bad habits.

Use the theory of constraints to your advantage and and I promise you’ll see your results soar through the roof.

What’s your constraint?
