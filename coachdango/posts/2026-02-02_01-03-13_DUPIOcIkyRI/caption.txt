My friend called me crying about his weight loss journey.

“I’m so tired of tracking calories. Why can’t this just be automatic?”

I let him finish. Then I said something that pissed him off:

“You’re not suffering. You’re privileged.”

Think about it:

Tracking calories? Means you have food to count.

Tired from workouts? Means you’re not doing backbreaking labor to survive.

Choosing what to eat? Means you have choices.

Your great-grandfather didn’t track macros.
He worried about putting food on the table.

You’re not burdened by fitness.
You’re blessed with abundance.

The “problem” of getting in shape is a luxury 99% of human history couldn’t afford.

My clients who get this mindset shift:
• Stop complaining, start appreciating
• Find joy in the process
• Get results 3x faster
• Never quit again

Here’s the truth:

Every workout is a celebration of what your body CAN do.
Every healthy meal is a privilege that billions don’t have.
Every choice is a gift.

Stop treating fitness like a punishment.
Start treating it like the privilege it is.

You GET to move your body.
You GET to choose your food.
You GET to transform.

Not everyone can.

Do you see fitness as a burden or a blessing?
