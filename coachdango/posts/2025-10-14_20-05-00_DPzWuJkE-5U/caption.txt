“I can’t afford a trainer. I don’t have time for the gym.”

Sarah had every excuse. 250 pounds. Pre-diabetic. Two kids.

But she had something stronger than excuses: She was tired of being tired.

Started with 5-minute walks. Then 10. Downloaded a free workout app. Did pushups against her kitchen counter while coffee brewed.

Six months later, she’s down 45 pounds. Off her pre-diabetes meds.

The biggest obstacle wasn’t her schedule. Wasn’t money. It was the story she told herself.

Your mind will quit 1000 times before your body does.

So my question to you today is:

What story are you telling yourself?

- Dan
