“I eat my stress away.”

My client Mark is the CFO of a startup.
Every merger, every deadline, every crisis = pizza order.

40 pounds overweight.
Pre-diabetic at 38.

I gave him one rule:
“When stress hits, hit the gym. Not the fridge.”

He laughed. “I don’t have time to workout when I’m stressed.”

“You don’t have time NOT to.”

We started simple:
Stressed = 10 pushups
Anxious = 5-minute walk
Overwhelmed = Hit the heavy bag

Week 1: “This is stupid.”
Week 2: “It’s... actually working”
Month 2: Down 15 pounds
Month 6: Off all meds

His revelation:
“Food made stress worse. Exercise killed it.”

Stress eating = Temporary relief, permanent regret
Stress lifting = Permanent relief, temporary discomfort

Your body needs to move stress out. Don’t eat it down.

- Dan
