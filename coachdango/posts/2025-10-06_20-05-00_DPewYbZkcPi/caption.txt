“I’m too old to start,” my client said at 40.

Today, at 44, he deadlifts and runs half marathons.

40 isn’t old. It’s barely halftime.

My 52-year-old client just got his black belt. 
My 48-year-old started her business. 
My 55-year-old ran his first Spartan race.

You’re not declining. You just stopped trying.

Your best decade starts when you decide it does.

So my question to you today is:

What are you starting today?
