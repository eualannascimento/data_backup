I gave this list to a client 6 months ago.
He called it “too extreme.”

Yesterday he texted:

“I feel like I’ve been asleep for 40 years.”

Everyone’s chasing hacks and shortcuts.
But the truth? The “secret” has been right in front of you:
Discipline × Time.

6 months feels long on day one.
Feels like nothing when you’re in a new body.

He lost 47 lbs.
Came off blood pressure meds.
Plays with his kids again.
Crushed his biggest deal ever.
His wife says he’s a new man.

The problem isn’t knowledge.
It’s that we’ve normalized feeling like shit.

You could be unrecognizable in 6 months, 
or still looking for shortcuts.

Your call.

- Dan
