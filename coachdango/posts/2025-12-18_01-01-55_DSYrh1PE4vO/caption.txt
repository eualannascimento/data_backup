6 rules to creating a fit body for life over the age of 40:

1. Restrain the Brain: Choose a form of restriction that feels least restrictive for you.

2. Keep Yourself Honest: Self-monitor through frequent weighing and food intake tracking.

3. Make It Sustainable: Don’t focus on speed. Focus on direction. Aim for gradual changes for better long-term results.

4. Understand the Body’s Defence System: Your body doesn’t like losing weight. So it will increase hunger and appetite the more weight you lose. Prepare for this.

5. Daily Activity: Engage in regular exercise. This improves satiety and sensitizes the brain to body signals.

6. Evolve Yourself: Form a new identity and lifestyle around your healthy habits. This is not about weight loss. It’s about becoming a better version of yourself.

Follow these evidence-based rules, and your body will thank you when you’re 50.
