I’m 45 and just beat my 25-year-old self in every fitness test.

Meanwhile, I know 25-year-olds who grunt getting off the couch.

The difference? They’re aging. I’m not.

Let me explain:

You have two ages fighting for control of your body.

Chronological age: The candles on your cake. Meaningless.
Biological age: How your cells actually function. Everything.

Science proved you can reverse biological age:

My 60-year-old client? Just deadlifted 315 for the first time.
Charles Eugster? Started bodybuilding at 87. Became a champion at 95.
Me? Blood work of a 28-year-old at 45.

How? Simple:
• Lift heavy things
• Eat real food
• Skip the cigarettes
• Limit the booze
• Challenge your brain
• Move every day

Most people use age as an excuse.
“I’m too old to start.”
“My metabolism is shot.”
“It’s just part of aging.”

You’re never gonna be younger than right now. This second. This moment.

So what’s it gonna be?

Make excuses about the candles?
Or blow them out with the lungs of someone half your age?

Your cells don’t know what year you were born. They only know what you did today.
