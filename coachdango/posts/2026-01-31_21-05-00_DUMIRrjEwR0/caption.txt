An estimated 60–85% of people in the world and nearly two-thirds of children lead sedentary lifestyles. 
 
Researchers found that one of the best ways to counteract this is to have a snack. 
 
Specifically, you want to have an exercise snack. 
 
They found that a 5-minute walk every 30 minutes could offset the effects of prolonged sitting. 
 
This can also be done in other ways, like: 
 
1. Parking farther away from your destination. 
2. Taking the stairs over elevators or escalators. 
3. Getting a walking treadmill for your desk. 
 
Sometimes, the idea of taking an hour to be active can be daunting for some people. 
 
Try getting little “exercise snacks” throughout the day.
