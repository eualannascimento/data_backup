One of the worst mistakes I made in the gym was spending hours a day, 7 days a week, trying to build muscle.

I thought you had to hit the gym and pound your body with weights until I could no longer lift my arms.

I was wrong.

Muscle growth happens when you rest.

Hitting the gym too many times a week slows down this process as you don’t give your body enough time to rest & recover.

The formula is:

Have a good workout + Fuel your body with good nutrition + recover with sleep/walking.

Do this enough times, and your body will end up thanking you.

So my question to you today is:

What do you think most people miss when trying to build muscle?
