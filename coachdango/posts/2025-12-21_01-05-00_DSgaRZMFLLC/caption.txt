For the next 7 days, walk 30 minutes. No phone. No music. Just you and a notepad.

My highest-performing clients do this daily. They solve million-dollar problems on suburban sidewalks.

Week 1: “This feels weird.”
Week 2: “Had my best idea in years.”
Week 3: “Can’t believe I lived without this.”

Your brain is drowning in inputs. Podcasts, audiobooks, notifications, content. Zero space to actually think.

The walk isn’t an exercise. It’s an excavation.

You’re digging out insights buried under everyone else’s noise.

Solutions to 3-year problems appear at minute 18.
Clarity about that toxic relationship hits at minute 22.
Your next big move reveals itself at minute 27.

Try it for 7 days. Or keep wondering why you feel stuck.
