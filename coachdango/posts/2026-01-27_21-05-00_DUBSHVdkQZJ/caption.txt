Here’s how to feel better at 40 than you did in your 20’s: 
 
1. Walk every day for 30 minutes a day. Preferably outside. This decreases stress, increases energy, and improves your mitochondrial health and capillary density. 
 
Do this every day and it’ll be the best part of your day. 
 
2. Eat a high-protein diet made of single-redient nutrient-dense foods. Aim for .8 grams of protein per pound of body weight. 
 
3. Hit the gym 3x a week for at least 30 minutes each session. Use the gym to build muscle and get stronger. 
 
4. Get 8 hours of quality sleep. Start with creating the right environment for sleep. Then work on your habits. Track your sleep using an Oura Ring or Whoop band. 
 
5. Create a habit of drinking water. Add some electrolytes while you’re at it. Do this in the morning and as a way to avoid snacking. 
 
Above all else invest in your body. When you do this the returns you get will be insane.
