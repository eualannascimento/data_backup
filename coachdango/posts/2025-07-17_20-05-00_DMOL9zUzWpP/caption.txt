They’re loaded with water, which helps fill your stomach fast.

They take time to chew, which gives your brain a chance to catch up with your stomach.

They also have pectin, which is a special fiber that turns into gel in your gut.

It slows down emptying, balances blood sugar, and feeds your good gut bacteria. 

Apples are nature’s natural appetite suppressant. 

The next time you feel like you want a snack, pause and ask yourself if you want to eat an apple. 

If you do, they are one of the best things you can do to control calories and stay full.
