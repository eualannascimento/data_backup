My client was a cardio junkie who avoided weights.

“I don’t want to get bulky,” she said.

6 months later, she PR’d her half-marathon by 12 minutes.

What changed? She started lifting heavy.

Here’s what most people miss:

Cardio helps weights:
- Better recovery between sets
- More training volume
- Stronger work capacity

Weights help cardio:
- Powerful stride
- Injury prevention 
- Faster finishing kick

She now squats her bodyweight. Runs faster than ever.

Her secret? They feed each other.

Strong legs = efficient running.
Strong heart = heavier lifts.

Stop choosing sides. Your body wants both.

The runners who lift outlast everyone.
The lifters who run outperform everyone.

My client learned this at 48. Now she’s unstoppable.

So my question to you today is:

Are you team cardio or team weights?
