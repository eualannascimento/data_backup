Japan is the 3rd richest country in the world, and has one of the lowest obesity rates.

It’s not genetics. It’s not magic. Its systems.

From age 6 to 18, every school in Japan has a nutritionist teaching kids how to eat well and make smart food choices.

While America throws meds at the problem, Japan builds healthy habits early.

It’s not willpower, it’s the environment.

Good food. Smart systems. Start young.
