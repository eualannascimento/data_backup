Life is a game of minimizing regrets.

You can’t see what’s going to happen later on, but you can do things now to enable inner peace later on.

For big decisions, ask yourself one simple question,

“Will I regret not doing this in 10 years?”

If the answer is yes, then take action.

· Get your body in shape
· Make your family the priority
· Chase impact over quick money
· Stay in touch with your friends
· Give yourself permission to stop worrying & be happy
· Have the courage to live a life true to yourself, not the life others expect of you

You can’t go wrong making those types of decisions as they will impact the quality of your life and others.

You can’t avoid regret, but you CAN minimize it.

And decisions are hardest to make when it’s a choice between what’s right and what’s comfortable.

My hope for you is that you make the right decisions now so you avoid regret later.
