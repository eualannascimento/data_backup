I live by one value: regret minimization.

If there’s a chance I’ll regret not doing something, I take action now.

Not tomorrow. Not “when the time is right.” Now.

Most people overestimate the risk of trying. They underestimate the pain of wondering “what if.”

The worst decisions aren’t the ones that fail. They’re the ones you never made.

That conversation you’re avoiding.
The business you want to start.
The change you keep postponing.

Future you is watching.

Will they thank you or resent you?

Stop playing it safe. Start playing it smart.

- Dan
