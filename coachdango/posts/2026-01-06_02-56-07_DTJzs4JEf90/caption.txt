Can I say something without everybody getting mad at me?

Your mental health is tied to your physical health.

What I’m not saying is that you will see no mental health issues as a result of being at a healthy body composition, and I’m not saying that either.

But if we want a chance at being mentally healthier, we must focus on:

- What we feed ourselves
- How we move our bodies
- How are we sleeping
- How often do we go out into nature

How do I know this? When I was a fat, directionless, mentally unhealthy high school dropout who was looking to change my life, the one thing that helped me was going to the gym.

I didn’t have control over much. I didn’t even have control over my own thoughts. What I did have control over was stepping through the gym doors every single day.

Making exercise a non-negotiable part of my life was what turned my life around.

And it’s NOT going to solve your problems. They will just make your problems easier to deal with so you can get the help you need to solve them.

- Dan
