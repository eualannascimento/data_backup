Weights or cardio?

Wrong question.

Weights = Your armor. Protects joints, builds bone, and prevents falls.

Cardio = Your engine. Cleans arteries, sharpens brain, keeps heart young.

One without the other is like driving a Ferrari with bicycle tires.

Science says:
- Just weights: 20% longer life
- Just cardio: 25% longer life 
- Both: 41% longer life

That’s not fitness advice. That’s math.

Your future self needs both.
