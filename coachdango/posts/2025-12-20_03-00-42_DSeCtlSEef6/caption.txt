Most people think that eating whole foods is a diet when it’s really just eating normally. 
 
I’ve got nothing against fast food, but if it’s the only thing you eat on a regular basis, then you’re inviting problems into your life down the road. 
 
When I went from a diet of ultra-processed foods to eating whole, single-ingredient, nutrient-dense foods, I was surprised at how lean & energetic I became. 
 
Don’t sleep on the fact that a simple switch can make a world of difference.
