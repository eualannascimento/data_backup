7 fitness cheat codes for people over 40: 
 
1. 10 minutes before a workout, eat 30 grams of carbs to boost your performance. 
 
2. Magnesium L-threonate helps you boost brain power and helps your body relax. 
 
3. Do a short dynamic warm-up before exercise to wake up your nervous system and prep it for a great workout. 
 
4. Do you sit a lot and find it hard to hit your steps? Invest in a treadmill desk. An easy way to get steps without thinking about it. 
 
5. Have limited time to workout? Use supersets. This is where you pair 2 non-competing exercises together and do them without rest. You can save up to 36% more time working out this way. 
 
6. If you want to recover faster, don’t sit on your butt. Go for a walk. This will stimulate blood flow and help your body feel less stiff and tight. 
 
7. Push your abs out like Mike Tyson was gonna punch them. This is called “bracing your core”. You do this to activate your core to gain more strength & protect your lower back.
