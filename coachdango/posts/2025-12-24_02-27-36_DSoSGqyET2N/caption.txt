Goals are for amateurs. Systems are for winners.

“I want to lose 20 lbs” is a goal.
Walking 10,000 steps daily is a system.

“I want to get stronger” is a goal.
Lifting 3x weekly at 6am is a system.

Goals depend on motivation. Systems run on autopilot.

My fittest clients never chase goals. They build systems so boring that they forget they’re doing them.

Same workout. Same meals. Same bedtime. 847 days straight.

The guy who “wants to get fit” quits in February.
The guy with a system is unrecognizable by December.

Stop setting goals. Start building systems.

Winners don’t rise to their goals. They fall to their systems.

- Dan
