If you spend over 6 hours sitting at a desk, read this:

Bad posture doesn’t just make you look bad, it affects your confidence and health.

Sitting all day tightens your hips, weakens your glutes, and rounds your shoulders.

This leads to a chain reaction of pain from the neck down to the lower back.

Here’s how to fix it:

→ Couch Stretch – Loosens tight hips (30-60 sec per side)
→ Facepulls – Better shoulder alignment (2-3x/week, 3x12-15 reps)
→ Dead Hangs – Decompress your spine (3x30 sec)
→ Hip Thrusts – Strengthens glutes for better posture (2-3x/week, 2x8 -15 reps)
→ McGill Big 3 – Bird Dog, Side Plank, McGill Crunch (6 reps each)
→ Walking – Boosts circulation, loosens joints, and supports your spine (20–30 min/day)

Give this routine 30 days and you’ll feel taller, stronger, and pain-free.
