Getting in shape isn’t about macros or meal plans.

It’s about identity.

You don’t need better tactics.

You need a better question:
“What would the healthiest version of me do right now?”

That’s it.

When you operate from that identity, habits become automatic.

The weigh-ins don’t matter.

The spreadsheets don’t matter.

Because you’re no longer forcing behaviors.

You’re living them.

Most people change tactics but keep the same identity.

Then wonder why nothing sticks.

Change who you are first.

The results follow naturally.

What does the best version of you do today?

- Dan
