Sitting all day is one of the most underrated health risks. It wrecks your metabolism, tanks your energy, and increases your risk of disease.

A new study just showed that doing 10 bodyweight squats every 45 minutes during an 8-hour workday improves blood sugar regulation better than a 30-minute walk.

Why? Frequent squats activate your legs and glutes, which boosts glucose uptake and reduces blood sugar spikes more effectively than a single long workout.

Movement snacks like this might be one of the simplest ways to fight the damage of sitting, and upgrade your metabolic health.
