My client Rachel taught me the 20-minute rule that killed her snacking habit.

“I reach for chips every afternoon at 3pm,” she told me. 

We tried everything. Then she discovered this: Drink 500ml of water. Set a 20-minute timer. Get busy with literally anything else.

Week one, she forgot about the snack 6 out of 7 times. 

A month later? Down 8 pounds without feeling deprived once.

Turns out she wasn’t hungry. She was bored, dehydrated, or just following a pattern.

Your cravings aren’t commands. They’re suggestions.

So my question to you today is:

What’s your 3pm weakness?
