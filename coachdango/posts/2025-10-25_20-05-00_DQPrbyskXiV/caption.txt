Stop waiting to be “ready.”

There’s no perfect Monday. No ideal schedule. No magical motivation moment.

You start fat and weak. That’s the point.

Motivation isn’t the spark that starts the fire. It’s the heat that comes after you light the match.

Every fit person you admire started exactly where you are. Scared. Uncomfortable. Making excuses.

The difference? They started anyway.

Your first workout will suck. You’ll be sore. You’ll want to quit.

Good. That’s growth.

The pain of starting fades in weeks.
The regret of waiting lasts forever.

Start today. Start ugly.

- Dan
