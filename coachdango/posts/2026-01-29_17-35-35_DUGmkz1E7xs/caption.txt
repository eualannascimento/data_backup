Stop eating 3-5 hours before bed.

Not for fat loss. For the domino effect it creates:

→ Better sleep quality
→ Fewer wake-ups at night
→ Balanced hunger hormones
→ Improved digestion
→ No more late-night binges when willpower crashes

Your body has a rhythm. Eating whenever you want disrupts it.

And here’s the truth about intermittent fasting:

If you’re stressed, it might be making things worse. Not eating is still stressful on your body.

Stop fighting your biology. Start working with it.

Eat when your body wants fuel. Rest when it needs recovery.

Timing matters more than you think.
