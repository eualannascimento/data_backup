When I was younger, I thought status was the watch you have and the car you drive. As I’ve gotten older, I realize it’s none of that.

High status is about:

1. Being in good health
2. Being loved by your family
3. Having a relationship to something greater than yourself
4. Doing something you love that’s making a difference

You can’t buy this form of status. It must all be earned.

So if you’re hitting all of these marks right now, but you’re not driving the fancy car and don’t care to get a watch, I commend you.

(And if you’re into fancy things, I support you as well.)

Let’s keep our eyes on the great prize of life, which can be found in where you stand and things that cannot be bought.

- Dan
