The other day, I had a conversation with one of the top functional dentists in the country.

I told him about how I’ve evolved towards sleeping at 9 and waking up at 6 while having my meals at 5pm, which reminded me of my grandpa.

I asked him if we evolve as we age, but he had an interesting answer for me:

With age comes wisdom.

The more you get in tune with your body and develop good habits, the wiser you become.

If doing things like having great sleeping habits and eating early meals makes me feel amazing, then go ahead and call me grandpa.

- Dan
