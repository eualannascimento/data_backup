I was having a conversation with one of the best functional medicine doctors in America. 
 
He was a wise man in his 60s. I asked him whether tending to a garden was a sign of getting old. 
 
He told me that it’s a sign of wisdom more than age. 
 
Sometimes age comes with wisdom. Sometimes it doesn’t. 
 
It was a reflection of my appreciation for the simple yet profound things in life. 
 
And here I was thinking I was trying to grow strawberries to eat them with my family. 
 
Suffice it to say, we can (and should) look to our elders for wisdom more often. 
 
Some of them have this life thing figured out. 

- Dan
