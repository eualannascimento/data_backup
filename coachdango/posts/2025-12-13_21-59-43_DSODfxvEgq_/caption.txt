Getting your body in shape will teach you so many things about yourself that it becomes a self-development journey disguised as a physical one.

You’ll learn about your weaknesses, the areas where you use food and alcohol to cope, and the emotions you’re avoiding.

Most people think that it’s all about calories and macros and what weights to lift and what exercises to do, but that’s the simple part.

The hardest part is changing who you are and how you react to the world around you.

- Dan
