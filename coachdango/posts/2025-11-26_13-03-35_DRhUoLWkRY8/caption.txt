The biggest mistake I see people make when they’re trying to lose weight:

Losing Muscle.

This drives down your metabolism and can also lead to loss of bone density.

When you’re in a caloric deficit, your body lacks energy from food, so it starts breaking down fat and muscle for fuel.

Something that you have to understand is that muscle is hard to gain.

It sometimes takes months and even years for some people to regain the muscle they have lost when they lose weight.

The best way to combat this is to make sure you’re eating enough protein (.8 to 1g per lb) and lifting heavy weights. 

This will ensure that you keep your muscle and maybe build a little bit while maintaining a healthy metabolism.
