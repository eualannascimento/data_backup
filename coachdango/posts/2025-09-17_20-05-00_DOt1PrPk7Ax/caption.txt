My client tried buying his way to fitness. $10K biohacking equipment. Celebrity trainer. Ozempic prescription.

Nothing worked.

“You can’t purchase what must be earned,” I told him.

So he earned it. 5am workouts when he wanted to sleep. Meal prep when he wanted takeout. Saying no to drinks when deals were on the table.

365 days of showing up.

Now? He walks into rooms differently. Commands respect without speaking. Closes bigger deals because clients trust discipline.

“My body is my only real asset,” he says. “Everything else can be faked.”

Status isn’t bought. It’s built.

So my question to you today is:

What are you earning?
