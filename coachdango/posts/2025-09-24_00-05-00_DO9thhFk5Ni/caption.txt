A client landed in the ER after endless burpees, a herniated disc, $30k bills, and six months lost. 

Pain isn’t progress. After 40, burpees wreck spines, wrists, and shoulders. 

We swapped them for hip thrusts, swings, sleds, ropes. 

Now he’s fitter, pain-free, and playing with his daughter. 

Hard ≠ smart.
