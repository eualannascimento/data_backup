Trying to burn fat with your workouts is like trying to empty out a pool using a teaspoon.

Even if you do lose weight as a result of working out, it sets you back farther than you think, because you have to keep up the same intensity and capacity to keep the weight off.

When you focus on building muscle, you’re changing the game.

You may not burn as many calories while you’re working out, but every pound of muscle adds about 7 calories to your metabolism.

This may not seem like a lot, but over the course of a year or even decades, it compounds.

Add 5-10 pounds to your frame, and you have a fast metabolism, you’re more functional as you age, and you’re also looking pretty sexy.

So, stop trying to burn fat with your workouts. Use your diet for that shit. You’ll go a lot further with that mentality.
