The US Government came out with the new food pyramid and it's 100x better than the old one. Here's why:

The old pyramid told us to load up on bread, pasta, and cereal. It put fats at the tiny tip like they were poison.

Result? Obesity epidemic. Diabetes rates are through the roof. 77% of military-aged youth can't qualify for service.

The new pyramid finally gets it right:

• Prioritizes protein at every meal (1.2-1.6g per kg bodyweight)
• Full-fat dairy is back. Whole milk. Real cheese.
• Minimizes ultra-processed food intake
• Whole grains stay, but in the smallest portion
• Zero added sugar recommended for kids under 4

The core message is three words: Eat real food.

Not complicated macros. Not trendy diets. Real food.

Meat. Eggs. Vegetables. Fruits. Nuts. Olive oil.

This is what we've been telling clients for 20+ years. The government finally caught up.

The nursing home industry is $180 billion. The ultraprocessed food industry is even bigger.

You know what beats both? A grocery cart full of real food and 3 gym sessions a week.

Now, some people won't like the message because it came from RFK, and they're entitled to their opinions. 

But overall, this is a big win for Americans.
