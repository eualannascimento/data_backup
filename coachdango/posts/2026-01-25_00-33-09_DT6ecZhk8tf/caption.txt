One of the easiest ways to get in shape?
Set boundaries.

We live in a world of massive food abundance.

You can have gourmet meals or alcohol delivered to your door in 30 minutes or less. Any craving can be satisfied instantly.

That’s exactly why you need rules, especially around food.

No snacking: Stops you from eating out of stress or boredom.

Scheduled meals: Keeps your appetite steady and energy consistent.

Daily protein goal: Builds muscle and keeps you full.

Ancient foods only: If it existed 1,000 years ago, it’s probably good for you.

With abundance comes responsibility.
Boundaries create freedom.
