The key to avoiding the yo-yo cycle is to allow yourself sufficient time to get in shape.

I don’t mean 6 or 12 weeks. I’m talking 1-2 years.

The reason I say this is that the most counterintuitive secret is that the slower you go, the more you retain the results.

You give yourself time to begin behaviors and lifestyle, you’re also not rushing as much, which helps you avoid the common pitfalls and mistakes that people make when they’re trying to do it as fast as possible.

So, if you are going to get in shape this year, please, for the love of God, give yourself enough time.

Your future self won’t regret it.
