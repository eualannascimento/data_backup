France’s lower obesity rate isn’t about willpower, it’s about lifestyle. They walk more, eat slower, shop fresh, avoid snacking, and make meals social.

Want similar results?

• Walk daily
• Eat real food slowly
• Shop fresh, not in bulk
• Embrace hunger
• Make meals intentional

Change your environment, not just your diet.

So my question to you today is:

What’s one French habit you could adopt today?
