Why I would not get an overweight person to run for weight loss.

This is specifically about the pounds of pressure that they are placing on their knees when they are running long distances.

For reference:

For every pound you lose, it takes 4 pounds of pressure off your knees and 6 pounds off your hips.

A 5% reduction in body weight can result in a 50% reduction in joint pain.

Most people don’t have joint issues. They have weight issues.

So my question to you today is:

What exercise are you doing that’s hurting more than helping?
