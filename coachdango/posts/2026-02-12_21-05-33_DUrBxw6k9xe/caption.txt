I don’t admire billionaires as much as I do the people who have their shit together in life. 
 
Yes, making money is important. 
 
But what’s most important is balancing that with the things that matter in life. 
 
This may be just me, but I’m willing to leave a couple of million on the table if it means more time with my family and health. 
 
As the saying goes, 
 
“You can lose money and make it back. If you lose time, it’s gone forever.” 
 
How do you measure success in life?
