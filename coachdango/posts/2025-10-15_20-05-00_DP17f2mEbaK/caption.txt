“I want abs but hate boring workouts.”

My client Steve wanted the six-pack without the grind. CEO mindset.

I told him: “Getting in shape IS boring. That’s the point.”

Same breakfast. Same gym time. Same walks. Every. Single. Day.

He rolled his eyes. Started anyway.

6 months later? Down 30 pounds. Visible abs. But the real win?

“The boring stuff became automatic. Now I have energy for the exciting stuff.”

Success isn’t sexy. It’s showing up when you don’t feel like it.

Boring works.

So my question to you today is:

What boring habit changed your life?
