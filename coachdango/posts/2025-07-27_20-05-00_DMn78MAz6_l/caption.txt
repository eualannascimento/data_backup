The biggest mistake people make when trying to lose weight is not having systems. 
 
Every outcome you want is behind a system you haven’t created. 
 
For weight loss, here’s a simple system that’s worked for others that might work for you: 
 
Nutrition - Eat 3 nutrient-dense meals a day on a schedule. No snacking. 
 
Workouts - Schedule your workouts as if they are flights you need to catch. 
 
Sleep - Use the 321 method. 3 hours before bed, no food. 2 hours before bed, no liquids. 1 hour before no screens. 
 
The body you want is behind the system you’re not using. 
 
So stop setting goals. Start setting systems.
