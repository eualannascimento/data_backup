Client texted me on his 40th birthday: “I’m officially old.”

Texted me on his 41st: “I’m officially unstoppable.”

What changed? He stopped believing 40 meant decline.

Year one: Lost 35 pounds. Deadlifted 400. Ran his first 10K. Got promoted twice. Started dating again.

At 25, he was broke and ripped. At 35, rich and soft. At 41? Both successful AND healthy.

Your 40s are golden because you finally have the resources, wisdom, and discipline your 20-year-old self lacked.

You know what matters. You can afford help. You’ve learned patience.

Most people peak at 40. They just convince themselves otherwise.

♻️ Share with someone afraid of 40
