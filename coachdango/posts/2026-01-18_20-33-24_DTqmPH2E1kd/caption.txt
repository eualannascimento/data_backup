Getting in shape isn’t just about workouts or diets, it’s about managing your emotions.

Most people don’t eat because they’re hungry.
They eat because they’re stressed, bored, or lonely.

The irony? Processed foods only make your mood worse.

Next time you want to change your state:
→ Walk when you’re anxious
→ Lift when you’re angry
→ Celebrate with experiences, not food

To tame the mind, exhaust the body.

You can’t remove emotional eating, only replace it.
And movement is the best replacement there is.

- Dan
