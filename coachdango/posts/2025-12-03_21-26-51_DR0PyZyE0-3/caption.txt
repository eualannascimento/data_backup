Let me share with you something that has taken me a decade to learn:

Body:
Hit the gym 3x/week. That’s it.

Relationships:
Find someone whom you wouldn’t mind doing absolutely nothing with. Lock that person down.

Work:
Pick something you’d do for free. Then charge for it.

Daily execution:
Show up. Do the work. Ignore everyone else’s scoreboard.

The happiest people I know are not the ones with the most money. They’re the ones who figured this out.

We overcomplicate success:
- 47-step morning routines
- Optimization frameworks
- Life hacks and shortcuts
- Comparison games

The truth is boring:

Lift heavy things. Love good people. Build something meaningful. Have more fun than everyone else.

That’s the whole playbook.

The person having the most fun always wins. Because they’re the only one who knows what game they’re actually playing.

Stop collecting tactics. Start living.

Period.

♻️ Share with someone making life harder than it needs to be.
