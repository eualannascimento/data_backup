One of my biggest mistakes was using workouts as punishment for what I ate.

When I was “bad,” I’d crush my body in the gym out of guilt.

Then I met Mike, a 60-year-old in incredible shape.

When I asked about his motivation, he said:

“I work out because I’m grateful for what my body can do.”

That hit me hard.

I stopped punishing myself and started training in gratitude.

Now I move because I can, not because I have to.

Your body isn’t something to fix.
It’s something to be thankful for.

- Dan
