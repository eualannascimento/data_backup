Want to win at life?

Do the opposite of average.

The average person:
- Gets 4,000 steps a day
- Eats processed garbage
- Sleeps 6 hours poorly
- Lives under artificial light
- Is anxious, broke, and lonely

High performers?

They reverse every single habit.

They move daily. Eat real food. Prioritize sleep. Get sunlight.

This isn’t complicated.

Average is a blueprint for what NOT to do.

Mark Manson said it best: The average person is unhealthy, in debt, and miserable.

So when you’re lost?

Look around at what everyone else is doing.

Then run the other direction.

Excellence isn’t mysterious.

It’s just the opposite of ordinary.

- Dan
