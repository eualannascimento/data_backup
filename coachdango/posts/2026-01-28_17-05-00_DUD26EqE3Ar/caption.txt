Eating whenever you want goes against how your body is designed to function.

Your circadian rhythm isn’t just about sleep. It controls digestion, too.

Stop eating 3-5 hours before bed. Not for fat loss, for the downstream domino effect:

→ Deeper sleep
→ Fewer nighttime wake-ups
→ Balanced appetite hormones
→ Better digestion
→ Less late-night overeating (when willpower is weakest)

And if you’re high-stress? Intermittent fasting might be backfiring.

Not eating is still stressful on your body.

Instead of restricting, align your meals with your biology.

Eat when your body is primed to digest. Rest when it’s primed to recover.

Work with your rhythm. Not against it.
