My client lost 40 pounds by being boring as hell.

Same breakfast. Same lunch. Same workout time. Same bedtime.

“I feel like a robot,” she complained after month one.

Month three: “This is the easiest thing I’ve ever done.”

The secret? She stopped deciding. Just executed.

No meal plan drama. No workout variety. No weekend “cheat days” that turn into cheat weeks.

Boring killed her decision fatigue. And her belly fat.

Most people want excitement. Winners want results.

So my question to you today is:

Ready to embrace boring for 90 days?

- Dan
