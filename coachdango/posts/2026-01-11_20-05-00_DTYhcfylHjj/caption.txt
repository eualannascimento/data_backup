Cardio is a terrible fat loss strategy.

Yes, it’s great for your heart and lungs. But for burning fat? It’s overrated.

Here’s why:

It burns fewer calories than you think. Your body adapts fast. It spikes appetite, you eat back what you burned. Too much kills muscle and wrecks joints.

Worst part? You’ll need to maintain that mileage forever just to keep the weight off.

The better approach:

Lift weights to build muscle and boost metabolism. Walk daily for sustainable fat burn without stress.

It’s easier to not eat 100 calories than to burn 100 on a treadmill.

Work smarter, not harder.
