Most people are fasting the wrong way. 
 
Here are a few reasons why... 
 
1. When you eat a high-protein meal first thing in the morning, you set your body up to build more muscle. 
 
2. When you have an early breakfast, you’re able to sleep better because your body doesn’t have to digest too much food at night. 
 
3. Sometimes, when people fast during the day, it increases appetite during the night, leading to severe munchies. 
 
4. Not eating is a stress. If you work a high-stress job, you don’t want to add more stress by fasting. 
 
5. Eating early in the day and fasting at night is more conducive to having a healthy circadian rhythm. 
 
6. You have better workouts with fuel in your stomach than without it. 
 
If you still want to skip breakfast, then by all means go ahead. 
 
Just know that there are better ways to go about it.
