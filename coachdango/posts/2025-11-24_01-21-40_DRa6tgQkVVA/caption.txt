The top 5 types of exercises to improve your mental health:

1. Boost Your Mood with Cardio

Aerobic exercise like running or cycling increases endorphins, reducing stress and immediately improving your mood.

2. Build Resilience through Strength Training

Lifting weights isn’t just physical; it’s mental. Pushing through sets helps build mental toughness, which can translate to better stress management.

3. Practice Mindfulness with Yoga

Yoga combines movement with deep breathing, lowering cortisol levels and helping you stay present, reducing anxiety, and improving mental clarity.

4. Increase Focus with HIIT: High-intensity interval training (HIIT) forces you to stay engaged and in the moment, sharpening your focus and combating mental fatigue.

5. Become happier through dance

Dancing is stress-reducing and helps with emotional regulation while endorphins are released. It’s one of the best feel-good cocktails on the planet.

Do any of these on a regular basis, and your body (and mind) will thank you.
