“When can I start eating normally again?” 

A client asked me this halfway through their transformation, and it revealed everything wrong with how we view fitness. 

Here’s the truth: there is no “after.” 

Getting in shape isn’t a 12-week sprint with a finish line. 

For most people, “normal” is exactly what got them out of shape, endless takeout, stress eating, and zero intention behind their choices. 

If you go back to normal, you go back to where you started. 

Real transformation isn’t about hitting a number on the scale. It’s about becoming a different person. 

You don’t just achieve fitness. You embody it. 

This isn’t a challenge. It’s a lifestyle.

What would change if you stopped treating health like a phase and started treating it like who you are?
