My client walked 10k steps, hit the gym daily, and ate “healthy”, yet gained weight.

Tracking revealed 3,550 calories on a 2,400-calorie maintenance. 

You can’t out-train your fork. Physics doesn’t care about effort, only deficit. 

We cut to 2,200 calories 8 weeks later, 16 lbs gone. 

Same effort, smarter eating.
