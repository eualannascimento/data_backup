5 Fat loss cheat codes that actually work:

1. Drink water before and after a meal to improve feelings of fullness from each meal.

2. Use the 80/20 diet where you get 80% of your calories from whole, nutrient-dense sources and 20% from whatever you want.

3. Walk after each meal to improve feelings of fullness and digest a meal.

4. Take L-carnitine. This transports fatty acids into the cell mitochondria for energy production and has been shown to reduce body weight modestly.

5. Be okay with going to bed a little hungry. Doing this is a good sign that fat loss is happening.

Try these out, and you may be looking at a leaner version of yourself in 2026.
