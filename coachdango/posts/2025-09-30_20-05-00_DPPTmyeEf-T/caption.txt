My client David waited 15 years to get fit.

“I’ll start when the kids are older.”
“After this project ends.”
“When work slows down.”

At 48, he had a mild heart attack. His doctor said the next one might kill him.

That was his wake-up call. Started walking that same day. Added weights the next week. Changed his diet immediately.

Six months later, he’s down 35 pounds and off three medications.

“I wasted 15 years waiting for the perfect time,” he told me. “There isn’t one.”

Start today. Your future self depends on it.

So my question to you today is:

What are you waiting for?
