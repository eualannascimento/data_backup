I heard something recently that absolutely gutted me.

A friend’s dad worked his whole life. Dedicated everything to his career.
He retired at 65. Passed away at 66.

On his deathbed, his son asked if he had any regrets.
He said, “I wish I had taken better care of my body.”

If you could fast-forward to that moment, and were offered 5–10 more healthy years, would you take it?

You have that chance right now.

Eat better. 
Move daily. 
Sleep well. 
Take care of your body.

Because one day, you’ll realize it was never about time management.
It was about energy management.

- Dan
