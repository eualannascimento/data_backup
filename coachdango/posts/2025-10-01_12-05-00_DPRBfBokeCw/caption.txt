I’m turning 46. The 33 micro-habits that transformed my life weren’t dramatic.

Drinking water before coffee. 
Walking during calls. 
Protein with breakfast. 
Reading 10 pages daily. 
Lifting three times weekly. 
Sleeping by 10pm. 
No phone in the bedroom. 
Sunlight before screens.

Small changes compound. My 25-year-old self pursued massive transformations, but failed repeatedly. My 45-year-old self made tiny adjustments and became unrecognizable.

The secret? Stop trying to change everything. Change one percent of everything instead.

Big goals die. Micro-habits live forever.

So my question to you today is:

What’s your smallest habit with the biggest impact?

- Dan
