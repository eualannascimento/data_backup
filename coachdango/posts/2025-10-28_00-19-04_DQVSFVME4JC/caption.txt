Then his doctor showed him the scan. Visceral fat wrapped around his organs like bubble wrap.

“This isn’t cosmetic,” the doctor said. “This is killing you from the inside.”

David was 0.58. Heart attack territory.

That belly fat isn’t just sitting there. It’s pumping inflammation into your bloodstream 24/7.

Six months later: Ratio down to 0.48. Doctor shocked.

So my question to you today is:

What’s your waist-to-height ratio?
