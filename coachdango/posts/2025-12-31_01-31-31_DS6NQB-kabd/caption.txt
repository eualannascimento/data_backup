Back in high school, I was a chubby, directionless kid who played video games during the day and binge-drank at night.

The thing that changed my life? The gym.

I wasn’t even supposed to go in the first place. My dad had given my brother a month-long membership, but he didn’t want it, so he gave it to me.

When I started going to the gym, it felt like my brain transformed.

I began having more positive thoughts. I started liking what I saw in the mirror. That boosted my confidence and self-esteem, and the rest is history.

I don’t know who needs to hear this, but don’t underestimate what exercise, sleep, and diet can do for your life.

Your physical health is deeply tied to your mental health.

Once you understand that, you unlock a whole new level.

- Dan
