Getting in shape is irrelevant if you’re not committed to changing who you are.

You can’t be the person who skips workouts and have abs.
You can’t eat like trash Monday-Friday and look good on weekends.
You can’t stay up scrolling until 2am and have energy.

The body you want requires becoming someone different.

Someone who meal preps on Sundays.
Someone who says no to happy hour.
Someone who treats sleep like a weapon.

Your old identity got you here. It won’t get you there.

Transformation isn’t about willpower. It’s about becoming someone who doesn’t need it.

So my question to you today is:

Who do you need to become?

- Dan
