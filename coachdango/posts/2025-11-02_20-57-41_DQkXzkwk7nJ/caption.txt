A silent luxury is having zero health problems.

My client Sarah realized this at 47, sitting in her corner office, making $500K, while taking 6 medications daily.

“I have everything except my health,” she told me.

We started simple:
Morning walks. Real food. Consistent sleep. Strength training 3x weekly.

9 months later:
- Off 5 medications
- 40 pounds lighter 
- Energy of her 30s
- Zero health issues

She still has the corner office. But now she says her greatest wealth is waking up pain-free, medication-free, and actually living, not just existing.

Health is the ultimate luxury.

So my question to you today is:

What would you trade for perfect health?
