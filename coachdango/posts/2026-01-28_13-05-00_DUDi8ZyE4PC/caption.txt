Going to bed a little hungry is one of the best signs your cut is actually working.

Not starving. Not lying awake with your stomach screaming at you. 
Just... a gentle emptiness.

That feeling means you created a real deficit today. Your body will spend the night tapping into stored energy instead of processing a late meal.

Most people fear this sensation. They snack it away the moment it shows up.

But hunger isn’t always an emergency. It’s information.

A little hunger at bedtime? That’s the tax you pay for progress. And honestly, you’ll sleep fine.

You’ll wake up fine. And over time, you’ll look in the mirror and see exactly why it was worth it.
