7 powerful ways to improve your health that don’t cost you a dime:

1. Do some breathwork
2. Get morning sunlight
3. Practice nasal breathing
4. Take a 15-30 minute walk
5. Talk to yourself positively
6. Cultivate a gratitude practice
7. Stretch between long desk sessions

Think of these as the “compound interest” of health. The more you do, the more the benefits add up.
