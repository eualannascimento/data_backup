I’ve noticed 3 non-negotiables in every high performer I coach.

First: They read daily.

Not scroll. Read.

Books expand thinking. You live a thousand lifetimes through other people’s lessons.

Second: They move their body.

Nearly every day. Even just 30 minutes.

Movement isn’t about abs. It’s about brain performance, clarity, and energy.

Third: They build systems.

Health. Time. Work. All on autopilot.

Systems make decisions. Decisions drain energy.

Average people rely on motivation.

Elite performers engineer their environment.

Which system are you building first?

- Dan
