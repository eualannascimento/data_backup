The other day, I had a conversation with a client, and something he said shocked me. 

He told me that getting in shape is a fight against modern-day society. 

We have made life so comfortable that all the things that make us feel human are the very things that we are or have been designed to avoid. 

And we have replaced that with simple pleasures. 

So when you are trying to get in shape, you have to understand that at the start, it’s going to be an uphill battle. 

But on the other side is feeling more energy, having a better brain, and feeling like yourself again, and possibly even feeling happier.

I promise you it’s worth it. You just have to try.

- Dan
