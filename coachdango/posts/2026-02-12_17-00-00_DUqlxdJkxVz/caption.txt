Most people fail because they want five years of damage fixed in six weeks.

It won’t work.

A client wanted to lose 20 pounds in six weeks.

I asked him, “Could you build your seven-figure business in six weeks?”

He laughed. “Not without cutting corners and burning out.”

Exactly.

You can’t out-sprint years of bad eating, poor sleep, and zero movement.

Crash diets trigger hunger. Old habits. Burnout.

Give yourself six months, not six weeks.

Build systems. Rewire behavior.

The goal isn’t just losing weight.

It’s becoming someone who keeps it off.

Stop chasing quick fixes.

Start playing the long game.

What would change if you gave yourself six months instead of six weeks?

- Dan
