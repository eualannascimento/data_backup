One of the best tips I can give anyone who wants to lose weight this year is to be friends with their hunger.

When you’re getting lean, you’re taking in less food.

Your body doesn’t like this, especially if prior to that, it’s been getting a steady stream of food whenever it wants.

All sorts of hormones (leptin, ghrelin, cortisol) can increase hunger as a way of protecting the body from “starvation”.

Instead of fighting this, learn to accept this as a short-term reality.

Eventually, the hunger goes away, and you’ll know it’s not always a sign to eat.

And when all else fails, drink water or eat some raw veggies while you tough it out.
