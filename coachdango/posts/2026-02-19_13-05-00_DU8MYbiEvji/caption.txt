Sometimes you need to ease into things. Other times, you need to go to extremes.

Doing a hard reset is going to the extreme in order to create momentum in your life.

The trick is to give yourself a period of time where you incorporate some (or all) of the habits.

During this time, you would journal the process to see what effect it has on your life.

I did this when I started on my Monk Mode journey in September.

I got rid of all alcohol, video games & distractions so I could pour my energy into my business.

After giving up those vices, I realized what place those vices had in my life.

Most times, I was using them to numb stress & anxiety, only to have it come back even stronger.

That hard reset period taught me a lot about myself, and I’m thankful I did it. It’s changed how I approach life.

What needs a hard reset in your life? What can you do to create momentum in the direction you need?

Sometimes it’s okay to ease in. Other times it’s better to go to extremes.

Imagine doing a life reset: what’s the first thing you’d stop doing today?
