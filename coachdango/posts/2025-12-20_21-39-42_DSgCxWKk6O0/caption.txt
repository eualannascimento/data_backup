10 things you should be able to do when you hit 80 years old:

1. Hike 1.5 miles on a hilly trail
2. Get up off the floor under your own power, using a maximum of one arm for support
3. Pick up a young child from the floor
4. Carry two five-pound bags of groceries for five blocks
5. Lift a twenty-pound suitcase into the overhead compartment of a plane
6. Balance on one leg for thirty seconds, eyes open (bonus: eyes closed for fifteen seconds)
7. Have sex
8. Climb four flights of stairs in three minutes
9. Open a jar
10. Do thirty consecutive jump-rope skips

Dr. Peter Attia calls this the Centenarian Decathlon, and while this may sound easy to you now, it’s a different game when you’re 80.

Want the ability to do all these at 80 and beyond?

Start making the investments in lifting, nutrition, and sleep now.

Your 80-year-old self will thank you.

So my question to you today is:

What do you think you’ll still be able to do at 80?
