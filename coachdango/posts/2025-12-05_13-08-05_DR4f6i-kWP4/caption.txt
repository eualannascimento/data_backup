I don’t know who needs to hear this, but people who track their food underestimate their calories by 20 to 50%.

They don’t account for the little bites, snacks, and finishing off their children’s food, not understanding that this all adds up.

@darrenliufitness and I had a great talk about this with @drkevinliu.

I’ve heard so many people cry about the fact that they were in a calorie deficit and not losing weight, when the truth is, they weren’t in a calorie deficit at all.

And this is not something to feel bad about. It’s just human nature.

So the next time you’re wondering why you’re not losing weight, look no further than the secret bites and snacks that you’re allowing into your life.
