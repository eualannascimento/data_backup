Believe you can have it all.

You can have a successful business/career while also having a healthy body and a family that loves you.

A lot is made of the trade-offs we have to make for the things that we want. I believe that’s a bunch of bullsh*t.

Trade-offs only happen when you don’t think intelligently enough about the life you’re trying to create.

You can have a fit body, tons of money in the bank, and a house full of love.

You just have to think deeply about integrating all parts to create the life you know you can have.

- Dan
