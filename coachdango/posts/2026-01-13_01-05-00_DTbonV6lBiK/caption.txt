That evening coffee is sabotaging your sleep.

Caffeine blocks adenosine, your brain’s natural sleep signal. Even if you fall asleep, you’re not getting deep or REM sleep. You wake up groggy. Reach for more coffee. Repeat the cycle.

Here’s what most people don’t know:

Caffeine consumed 6 hours before bed can cut your sleep by over an hour.

The fix is simple:

No caffeine within 10 hours of bedtime. Switch to herbal tea or decaf after noon.

Your sleep quality controls your energy, metabolism, and recovery.

Protect it as it matters. Because it does.
