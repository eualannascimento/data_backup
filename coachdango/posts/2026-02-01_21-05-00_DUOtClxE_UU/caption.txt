The best workout to improve cardio and gain muscle (that only takes 20-30 minutes):

They’re called Strength Circuits.

It’s the ultimate two-for-one deal for cardio and strength.

Here’s the breakdown:

1. Pick 3-4 strength moves (push-ups, squats, lunges)

2. Mix in 2-3 cardio bursts (jumping jacks, high knees)

Do each for 30-45 seconds with minimal rest between exercises. Repeat 2-4 rounds.

This high-intensity cocktail keeps your heart pumping while sculpting muscle.

It’s efficient, effective, and perfect for the time-crunched achiever. Remember, intensity is key.

Now go crush it!
