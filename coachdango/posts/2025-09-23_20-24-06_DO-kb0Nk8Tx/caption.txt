My client spent 10 years chasing abs. Then he changed his life instead.

Started waking at 5am. Built a morning routine. Cut the booze. Started walking meetings. Prioritized sleep over Netflix.

Never tracked a macro. Never counted calories. Just focused on becoming a different person.

6 months later: Down 35 lbs. Six-pack appeared. But that wasn’t the win.

His business doubled. Marriage transformed. Energy through the roof. The body was just a byproduct of the life change.

Stop chasing a fit body. Chase a fit life. The body follows automatically.

So my question to you today is:

What life change gave you the best body results?
