Everyone wants the pill. No one wants to do the work. 
 
Something I realized about getting fit is that it’s the ultimate display of the marshmallow test. 
 
Controlling what you eat takes willpower. 
 
Exercise sucks when you do it. 
 
Going to sleep on time requires discipline. 
 
But the payoffs to all of these are better health, focus, energy, creativity, and longevity. 
 
As Naval Ravikant says, 
 
“Hard choices, easy life. Easy choices, hard life.” 
 
My choice? I choose the hard, so my life becomes easier. 
 
How about you?
