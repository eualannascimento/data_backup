The other day, I saw a vegan say peanut butter is as good as steak when it comes to protein, but the math doesn’t work out: 
 
100 grams of peanut butter is 25 grams of protein at 588 calories. 
100 grams of steak is 25 grams of protein at 117 calories. 
 
Steak also contains micronutrients such as creatine, iron, zinc, selenium, riboflavin, niacin, vitamin B6, vitamin B12, phosphorus, pantothenate, magnesium, and potassium. 
 
Most jars of peanut butter contain processed sugars and oils. 
 
Even if you’re a vegan, there are way better protein sources like chickpeas, spelt & quinoa. 
 
Please repeat after me: Peanut butter is not a good source of protein.
