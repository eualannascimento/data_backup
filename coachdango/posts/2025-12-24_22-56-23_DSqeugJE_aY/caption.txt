I’m gonna say something that most 20-year-olds disagree with. But, screw it, I gotta say it anyways...

Being in shape in your 20s is expected, you’ve got minimal stress, optimal hormones, and plenty of time. 

But being fit at 40+ is rare and powerful. 

It means you’ve mastered discipline, time management, and consistency despite real-life responsibilities. 

You’re likely juggling a career, kids, and stress, yet still showing up for your health. 

That level of commitment signals high standards, self-respect, and long-term thinking. 

In a world where most let themselves go, staying fit at 40+ is one of the biggest status symbols on the planet.
