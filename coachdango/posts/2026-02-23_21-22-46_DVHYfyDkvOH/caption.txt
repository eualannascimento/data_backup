I’ve got no problem with making money.

It only becomes dangerous when it’s treated as an idol instead of a tool.

In your pursuit to improve your income, never lose sight of the things that truly matter in life.

Your health, values, family, and integrity are the things that make you wealthy. These cost way more than the dollars in your bank account.

Keep your eyes on what matters most. The funny thing is that when you do that, you can create a high income, but more importantly, a great life.

- Dan
