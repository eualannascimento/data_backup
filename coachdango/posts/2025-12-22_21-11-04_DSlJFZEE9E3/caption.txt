Most fit people have average genetics and above-average consistency in doing the boring things.

The thing people don’t realize is that good genetics doesn’t make you hit the gym consistently for years.

Your genetics don’t control the foods that go into your mouth.

Genetics don’t make you sleep on time.

So stop searching for the magic bullet to get in shape.

Start honing on doing the boring things consistently.

So my question to you today is:

What’s one boring habit you can stick to this week?
