I wasted years in the gym grinding 7 days a week. Here’s why that’s stupid:

I thought muscle required daily punishment. Arms shaking. Can’t lift tomorrow. That’s progress, right?

Dead wrong.

Muscle grows when you rest, not when you lift.

The actual formula:
• Intense workout (45-60 minutes)
• Quality protein (1g per lb bodyweight)
• Deep sleep (7-8 hours)
• Light walking on off days

That’s it. 3-4 gym days beat 7 exhausting ones.

Your muscles rebuild stronger during recovery. Skip rest, skip gains.

More isn’t better. Better is better.

So my question to you today is:

What gym mistake cost you the most time?
