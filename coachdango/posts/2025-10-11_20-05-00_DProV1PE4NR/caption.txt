“I’m doing everything right, but the scale won’t budge.”

My client Dave was stuck at 195 for 6 weeks. I had him photograph every single thing he ate for 3 days.

What we found blew his mind:

His “healthy” salad dressing: 300 calories
Coffee creamer (3x daily): 150 calories 
Olive oil for cooking: 250 calories
Kids’ leftover mac and cheese: 200 calories
Weekend wine: 400 calories

That’s 1,300 invisible calories. Every. Single. Day.

He thought he was eating 1,800 calories.
He was actually eating 3,100.

We didn’t change his diet.
We just made him aware of it.

Week 1: Down 2 pounds
Week 2: Down 3 more
Week 3: “I can’t believe it was that simple.”

The scale doesn’t lie. But your brain does.

Track everything for 3 days. Even the bites you “don’t count.”

Especially the bites you don’t count.
