Being fit in your twenties isn’t impressive.
Being fit in your forties is.

In your twenties, biology carries you.
Hormones high.
Recover fast.
Mistakes forgiven.

In your forties, everything is earned.
Stress higher.
Sleep matters more.
Muscle disappears faster.

Fitness becomes leadership.
Proof you manage energy.
Proof you control priorities.
Proof you keep promises to yourself.

Lift weights consistently.
Eat enough protein.
Walk daily.
Protect your sleep.

This isn’t about abs.
It’s about standards.

Fit men over forty aren’t lucky.

They’re deliberate operators building durable bodies for long-term performance.

Discipline compounds daily.

- Dan
