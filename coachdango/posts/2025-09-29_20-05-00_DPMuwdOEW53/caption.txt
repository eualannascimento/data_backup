My client weighed herself every morning for 10 years. The scale ruined her entire day.

Up 2 pounds? Skip breakfast. 
Down 1 pound? Celebrate with wine. 
Her weight yo-yoed while her happiness tanked.

I made her throw away the scale. “Focus on your habits, not the number.”

She started walking daily, lifting weights, and eating whole foods. Six months later, she’s down three dress sizes. Never stepped on a scale once.

Her weight? No idea. Her life? Completely transformed.

Stop chasing numbers. Start building a lifestyle.

So my question to you today is:

When did you last weigh yourself?

- Dan
