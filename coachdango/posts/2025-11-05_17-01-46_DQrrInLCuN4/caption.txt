You can eat clean, lift heavy, and still feel heavy inside.
That’s called emotional debt, and it compounds like interest.

Every time you suppress an emotion, you’re taking out a loan against your future peace. You might not notice it at first. But eventually, the body sends the bill: fatigue, stress, burnout, binge eating, anxiety.

You can’t out-train emotional debt. You can only feel it to free it.

The moment you stop trying to escape, you stop adding to the balance, and you finally start paying yourself back with awareness, truth, and compassion.
