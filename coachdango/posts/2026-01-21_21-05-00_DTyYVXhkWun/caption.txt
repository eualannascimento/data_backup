How to lose 30 pounds in 3 months. The simple science-based plan:

Nutrition:
→ Cut alcohol (it halts fat burning)
→ Calories: bodyweight × 11-12
→ Protein: bodyweight × 0.7g
→ First meal: 1-2 hrs after waking (30-40g protein)
→ Last meal: 3-5 hrs before bed

Hydration:
→ 500ml of water every morning
→ Drink between meals, not before bed

Workouts:
→ Strength train 3x/week, full body
→ Progressive overload weekly
→ Walk 8k-10k steps daily

Track:
→ 7-day weight average
→ Waistline + photos every 4 weeks

Getting fit isn’t about doing more. It’s about doing the right things consistently.
