8 hours. Same schedule. Track it. Most of you are running on 6 and wondering why you’re dead by 3pm.

10,000 steps minimum. Lift weights. Sprint once weekly. Motion creates energy. Sitting creates death.

4. Eat real food.
If it has a commercial, skip it. Nutrient-dense fuel only.

5. Get sun on your skin.
We’re solar-powered machines. 20 minutes daily. No excuses.

Age isn’t your problem. Your habits are.
